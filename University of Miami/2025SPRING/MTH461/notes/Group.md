### Definition 
A group is a set $G$ with a binary operation * such that
1. * is associative
2. there is $e\in G$ such that $e*x=x*e=x$ for all $x \in G$
3. for each $x \in G$ there is $y \in G$ such that $x*y=y*x=e$
### Examples
1. $(\mathbb{Z},+)$ 
2. $(Q,+)$
3. $(\mathbb{R},\cdot)$ 
4. $(\mathbb{Z},\cdot)$ - not group
5. $(Q,\cdot)$ not
6. $(Q-\{ 0 \},\cdot)$ is
